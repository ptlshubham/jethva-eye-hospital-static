About us

JETHWA EYE HOSPITAL

JETHWA EYE HOSPITAL provide services since 1983. Dr Girish Jethwa is Eye Surgeon. Laser surgery center, Phaco-Emulsification & Intra - Ocular lens implant specialist, Squint & Contact lens clinic, Diabetic Retinopathy clinic.

We will strive to ensure high level of patient satisfaction by providing comprehensive eye care of the best quality to every patient through compassionate and ethical approach regardless of his social or economical standing.
